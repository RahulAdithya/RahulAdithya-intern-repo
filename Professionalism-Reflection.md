### 1. Professional vs. Unprofessional Behavior
In a remote and casual environment like Focus Bear, professionalism is defined by **accountability** and **reliability** rather than formal dress codes.
* **Professional:** Communicating clearly when starting or finishing tasks, being punctual for meetings, and using appropriate emojis to convey tone and friendliness in text.
* **Unprofessional:** Disappearing for long periods without notice ("ghosting"), using aggressive or exclusionary language, or failing to acknowledge messages from teammates.

### 2. Respectful Remote Communication
* **Tone Management:** Since digital messages lack vocal cues, I will use clear, kind language and assume "good intent" from others.
* **Active Participation:** Using Discord "reactions" (like a checkmark or thumbs-up) to acknowledge receipt of information even when a long reply isn't necessary.
* **Constructive Disagreement:** Instead of shutting down ideas, I will ask clarifying questions like, "That's an interesting approach; how would we handle [X] scenario using that method?"

### 3. Giving and Receiving Feedback
* **Growth Mindset:** I view feedback as a tool for professional development. I will listen without becoming defensive and ask for specific examples to ensure I can improve.
* **Action-Oriented Feedback:** When giving feedback, I will focus on the work itself (e.g., "The loading screen lacks a progress bar") rather than the person, ensuring the tone remains supportive.
